Stat 1430 Outline

This page summarizes the major units, topics, and suggested OpenStax readings for Stat 1430 – Statistics for the Business Sciences.

Unit 1 — Data & Descriptive Statistics

  • Data, sampling, and observational studies
  • Observational studies vs. experiments
  • Graphing and summarizing data (numerical and graphical summaries)
  • Correlation and simple linear regression

Unit 2 — Probability Theory

  • Two-way tables and basic probability
  • Joint, marginal, and conditional probabilities
  • Independence
  • Bayes’ Rule

Unit 3 — Random Variables

  • Discrete random variables and their distributions
  • Expected value and standard deviation
  • Continuous random variables and density curves
  • Normal distribution and z-scores

Unit 4 — Sampling Distributions

  • Sampling distribution of the sample mean (x̄)
  • Sampling distribution of the sample proportion (p̂)
  • Central Limit Theorem

Unit 5 — Inferential Statistics

  • Confidence intervals for μ and p
  • Hypothesis tests for μ and p
  • t-distribution and inference when σ is unknown
  • Connecting interpretations back to real-world business decisions
